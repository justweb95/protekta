Dental implant placement

Diagnosis and treatment planning

Consultation with the implantologist, interpretation of your case, medical history, and radiographic images to find the best solution.

Surgery

The dental implant is surgically placed into the jawbone. This is a routine procedure performed under local anesthesia.

Healing

After the surgery, the integration time for the implant to bond with the bone is at least 3 months. During the healing process, a temporary tooth or bridge may be placed.

Final restoration

After the implants have integrated into the bone, the prosthetic work is created. The process takes 7-10 days.

Prosthetic work creation

Before any prosthetic work is made, an orthopantomogram, examination, and detailed treatment plan are required. If a tooth requires extraction, taking dental impressions and creating the prosthetic work is done 14 days after the extraction.
