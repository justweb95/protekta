First visit to the dentist

Before a child's first visit to the dentist, it is recommended that parents or guardians prepare the child at home.

Upon arrival, the child is introduced to the dentist, the clinic environment, and the instruments.

In some cases, a first dental examination may be conducted. If any treatment is necessary, it will be postponed to the next visit.

This approach helps the child get accustomed to the dental environment and reduces fear of future dental procedures.
